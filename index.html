Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Changhee Jung. My research focuses on computer architecture, with an emphasis on low-power design for intermittent processors. My current work aims to improve the overall energy efficiency of CPU microarchitecture by reducing the unnecessary energy waste in cache and memory. Additionally, I am also working on designing reliable and lightweight processors to address crash consistency issues arising from power interruptions. My solution allows the processors to recover from power interruptions without any software instruments and expensive hardware modifications.
