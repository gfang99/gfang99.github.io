Biography

I am a Ph.D. student of Computer Science at Purdue University, advised by Dr. Changhee Jung. My research focuses on computer architecture, with an emphasis on low-power design for intermittent processors. My current work aims to improve the overall energy efficiency of CPU microarchitecture by reducing the unnecessary energy waste in cache and memory. Additionally, I am also working on designing reliable and lightweight processors to address crash consistency issues arising from power interruptions.
