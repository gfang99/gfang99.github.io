Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Purdue University, advised by Prof. Changhee Jung. My research focuses on computer architecture, with an emphasis on low-power design for intermittent processors by revisiting and customizing existing architectural approaches. Besides, I am also working on designing reliable and lightweight processors to address crash consistency issues arising from power interruptions.
